About Me

Keyah Fuller is my name, and I am 15 years old. I am in my senior year of high school and live in Belize City, Belize.
What drove me to chose this issue is that we are in the midst of a pandemic, and while people are panicking and wondering, they are unconcerned about those who are homeless.
They, too, want assistance and are frequently disregarded. Even before the outbreak, they were chased away and unable to provide assistance.
People needed to be more aware of their existence and recognize its significance, as they were once like us.
